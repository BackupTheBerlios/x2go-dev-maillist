I did take a closer look:

The obfuscation addresses are delivered too. They say they are running
anti-spam against it, but most mailservers are running anti-spam so I
think it does not help. It can even give more identical spam because you
have more e-mail addresses in this way.

And I think people who prefer a secret mail address will use some Gmail
or Yahoo address on mailinglists, and not the real address.

So in my opinion it's better to use no obfuscation.

http://gmane.org/tmda.php
