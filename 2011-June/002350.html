I have just tested the most recent X2go TCE on my netbook yesterday  
and it used 41mb of its RAM.

The problem about low-RAM systems might be that the graphics adapters  
are probably pretty week, as well. This should in any case be avoided.  
Xorg can be a performance sucker, if the CPU or GPU is not fast  
enough. 64mb RAM sounds like rather week hardware, so please beware.
