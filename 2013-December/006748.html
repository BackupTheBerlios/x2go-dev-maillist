Package: x2gobroker-daemon
Severity: important

For easy use of x2gobroker-daemon with systemd, we need:

   o a service file (easy!)
   o a way to configure x2gobroker without  
/etc/default/{python-x2gobroker,x2gobroker-daemon}

The latter aspect is a little complex. Recommended approaches are  
drawn at [1].

For x2gobroker-daemon my suggestions is: provide a config file  
/etc/x2go/x2gobroker-daemon.conf in inifile format that gets parsed by  
<python-module>/x2gobroker/defaults.py. Environment variables have  
precedence over inifile options have precedence over the system  
defaults.

Mike

[1] http://0pointer.de/blog/projects/on-etc-sysinit.html
