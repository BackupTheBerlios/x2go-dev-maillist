serious DB engine like postgres instead?

http://wiki.x2go.org/doku.php/wiki:advanced:multi-node:x2goserver-pgsql

to everyone (I don't know - I'm taking your word for granted here), 
maybe the access rules on the postgres DB are stricter by default, or 
could at least be set in a stricter way without harming X2Go use?

Just a thought.
