Hi Stefan,

I would like to say that "I feel your pain." I agree with you that not
only are 1, 2 and 3 legitimate bugs/issues with X2Go Client, but so
are 4 and 5.

However, as the new maintainer of the X2Go Client package for Windows,
I do not believe that any of these bugs and issues are worth delaying
the 4.0.1.2 release to fix. Maybe if 1,2 and 3 were regressions
against 4.0.0.3, I would reconsider. However, they are existing bugs.

Fortunately, the x2go bug tracker exists not just to documents bugs
and how they will be fixed in future versions, but also to document
workarounds for current versions. I will try to identify workarounds
for you and I will post them on the bugtracker. I encourage you to do
this also.

Also, note that I fixed bug #230 specifically because it was easy for
me to fix and because the risk of a regression is very low. All I had
to edit was the NSIS script. I will need to brush up on C++ before I
can fix most x2goclient bugs.
