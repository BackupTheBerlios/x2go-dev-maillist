Hi Heinrich, hi all,

I have just added MD5, SHA1 and GPG signatures to all X2Go source  
tarball and binary downloads.

@Developers, esp. Alex:

The commands to use in the future for new uploads on

   http://code.x2go.org/releases

are:

   $ md5sum $tarball > $tarball.md5
   $ sha1sum $tarball > $tarball.sha1
   $ gpg -a -b --sign $tarball

The uploading developers sign the uploaded tarballs with their own GPG  
keys so.
