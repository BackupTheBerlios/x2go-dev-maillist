Package: x2goclient
Version: 4.0.1.0
Severity: minor

On Mac X2goclient will be shown in the language chosen at the time of
installing OS X (system wide). If the user chose danish as the language
upon first starting the machine, and then later changes the language to
english for his account, x2goclient will still be in danish, despite
everything else he starts being in english.
