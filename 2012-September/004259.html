Package: x2goclient

Severity: normal

Version 3.1.1.3

 
 
 
Sometimes a session is improperly terminated, but keeps the status 'R' this prevents the start of a new session. 

 
After investigating further I found x2goagent was running but could not be canceled normally. So I ran kill -9. After that the session disappeared and everything turned back to normal. So apparently there can be circumstances that x2goagent hangs.

 
In fact there are 2 issues here

 
1. x2goterminate-session should be able to handle a situation where x2goagent hangs and cannot be terminated normally

2. it is possible to get in a situation where x2goagent hangs. (I have no idea how that is done)
