Package: x2goclient
Version: 3.99.3.0-prerelease
Severity: minor

The x2goclient GUI in its middle (where the session log details can be  
viewed) has a couple of buttons, the most on the left is for  
,,terminate session''.

With DirectRDP, this button does not terminate the session, it simply  
terminates the RDP session. This is equal to a ,,suspend session'' call.

Does RDP logic have a mechanism to terminate a session from outside  
the session? If so, this should be implemented. If not, we should  
grey-out that button or show a warning or similar.
