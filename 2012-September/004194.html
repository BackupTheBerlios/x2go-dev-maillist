Hi all,

I have just added audio support for RDP sessions proxied through X2Go.

Packages for testing are currently building for Debian (heuler area),  
Ubuntu packages (ppa:x2go/ppa) will follow soon.

To enable audio in X2Go-proxied RDP session you only have to enable  
pulseaudio in the client's session profile manager.
