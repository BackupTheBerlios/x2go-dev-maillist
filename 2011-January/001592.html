Since there is a discussion on this topic...

*Launchpad <https://launchpad.net/>* would seem to be a reasonable fit for
hosting the x2go project as there are over 20,000 projects hosted on
Launchpad already?

Launchpad provides:

  - *Bug tracking* <https://bugs.launchpad.net/>
  - *Code hosting* <https://code.launchpad.net/> using Bazaar<
http://bazaar.canonical.com/>
  - *Code reviews* <https://help.launchpad.net/Code/Review>
  - Ubuntu package building and hosting <https://launchpad.net/ubuntu>
  - Language Translations <https://translations.launchpad.net/>
  - *Mailing lists* <https://help.launchpad.net/Teams/MailingLists>
  - *Answer tracking and FAQs* <https://answers.launchpad.net/>
  - Specification tracking <https://blueprints.launchpad.net/>

Click here for their tour <https://launchpad.net/+tour>

I think it would be very suitable for all debian related distro users.
