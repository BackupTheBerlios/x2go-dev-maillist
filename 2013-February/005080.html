I have tracked down the problem to line 41 in the source:
debian/x2gobroker-authservice.init:

  40 if ! getent group $X2GOBROKER_DAEMON_GROUP 1>/dev/null 2>/dev/null; then
  41         X2GOBROKER_DAEMON_USER=nogroup
  42 fi

It should set X2GOBROKER_DAEMON_GROUP, not X2GOBROKER_DAEMON_USER.
