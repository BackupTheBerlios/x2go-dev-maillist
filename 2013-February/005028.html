Hi Terje,

I suppose you don't need this to often, but very quick lesson in git: If
you come up with a similar situation in the future just commit to your
local git and run "git format-patch HEAD^". That will create a patch,
that represents what you are going to push. Running "git reset --hard
HEAD^" you can always go back to the previous version.
