You can also place a wrapper script /usr/bin/x2goagent that executes  
nxagent (with all cmdline options).

However, you will not have the X2GO splash then and neither will you  
have xinerama support and other features that X2Go introduces to NX  
(redistributed).

Using nxagent from another source is not be supported by us. You will  
be on your own if you take that path. ;-)

You can obtain NX (redistributed) here:
http://code.x2go.org/releases/source/nx-libs/
