I found a solution, but maybe it is not optimal. After your hint that 
this problem could be related to xorg, I activated the xorg-edgers 
repository in ubuntu tweak. From there one can get the newest x-packages 
for ubuntu. After upgrading the plugin is working now. So this problem 
seems really to be related to xorg. I hope there will be soon a fixed 
libX11-6 package in the official ubuntu repositories.
Thanks for your help!

Soeren
