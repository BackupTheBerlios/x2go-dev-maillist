Have you tried disabling (UNchecking) "Use SSH port forwarding to
tunnel sound system connections through firewalls"?

I have found that all versions of Windows x2goclient after 3.01-13,
including the latest 3.99.1.0 (preview2), have a problem with
tunneling sound through firewalls. It will intermittently lock up.
Sometimes it works fine for a while, but eventually the sound always
locks up. Disabling tunneling fixes that issue for me.
