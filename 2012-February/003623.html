Hi all,

as the traffic on the x2go-dev mailing list is currently growing  
enormously (which probably is partially my fault, as well ;-) ), this  
shows me that X2Go is in the discussion and also being used at your  
sites. That is very great. The dynamism is really lovely.

However, also the development team and the number of contributors is  
growing. The number of incoming patches has measurably increased  
within the last two months. That is awesome and we are very happy to  
observe this.

Meanwhile, this x2go-dev list is turning into a ,,real'' development  
list. We more and more discuss coding issues, send in / review  
patches, etc.

So, I (and also other developers) have to become more strict when  
discussion threads run off-topic for a development list. Please  
consider your topic well that you post to x2go-dev, make sure it is a  
development issue, a bug, etc.

However, issues that relate to using X2Go, fine-tuning your setup,  
etc.pp. please consider sending to the x2go-user mailing list. Please  
support us with our work by using the x2go-user list for issues on  
using X2Go. Let us keep this channel free for development.

Thanks to all for your great and continuous contributions,
Mike
