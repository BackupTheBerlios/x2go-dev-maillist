We have to make sure that no confusion occurs if some hostnames are  
hostname-only and some are FQDNs.

Proposal:

   o if all subdomains of multi-node servers are equal -> normalize to
     hostname-only
   o if subdomains of multi-node servers differ -> try to detect subdomains
     for all hostnames being in use (not always possible)

The more pragmatic case will be the first.
