I saw your discussion on IRC about building NX. For the client-side of  
X2Go you only need nxcomp and nxproxy. This we ship as  
nx-libs-lite-<version>.tar.gz.

Do not build the full nx-libs stuff. It is fine-tuned for being built  
on linux-only atm.
