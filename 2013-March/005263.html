Hi there,

I'd like to re-discuss our  registration policy. While I do agree that
spam is a problem, at least I refrained from fixing mistakes in wikis
several times because the registration was too complicated, or because
it took days to get an account and by that time my motivation was gone.
Thus I like to suggest to reopen the open user registration. To limit
the spam I suggest adding a captcha plus auto-generating the password.
I'd be willing to take care of the spam.
