More important is maybe that there is a webinterface for the mailinglist
now on: http://news.gmane.org/gmane.linux.terminal-server.x2go.devel
You can even post new messages to the maillinglist from there.

It would be nice if there comes a link on the x2go website to the
webinterface.

And you can access the mailinglist with a newsreader, by adding the
newsserver "news.gmane.org" and subscribe the newsgroup
gmane.linux.terminal-server.x2go.devel .
You can post messages there too, and replies are automatically to the
group (that's nice if you use Thunderbird).

If you want to post something you will have to confirm the first
message. That's against spammers.
