Hello, all.  We are in the home stretch of our first major X2Go
deployment and have hit a problem with using the x2go cups printer to
print to local devices.  When Windows users print to the X2Go printer
and select a physical printer from the list of local devices, we find
the print job is opened by whichever application is configured to open
PDF files.

For example, we had one station where the print jobs opened in Acrobat
Standard no matter what printer we sent the job to.  We removed Acrobat
Standard and the job now opened in Acrobat reader.  I'm assuming this is
a bug.  Is it something I have misconfigured? We really want the job
going where we send it and not to a PDF reader (unless, of course, we
tell it to go to the PDF reader!).  Thanks - John
