Dear friends of x2go,

we'd like to invite you to visit us on Linuxtag 2010 (9-12 June) in 
Berlin. You'll find us on our booth or you can visit our talk which is 
part of the debian miniconf 2010:

http://wiki.debconf.org/wiki/Miniconf-LT-Berlin/2010

For everybody outside Germany: The Linuxtag is a famous Open Source 
event which exists since 1996. It is a big event with a big focus on 
community (and - sure - on companies too -> where com meets org).
It is worth a visit if you want to contact the real people behind the 
projects.

We'll show the upcoming release, the plugin, as always our mascot and 
we'll answer your questions.

see you in berlin,
