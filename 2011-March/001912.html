I just had some trouble with my alt-gr-key. There reason was, that after
the update to squeeze the server had a different xmodmap then my client
(
http://daniel-lange.com/archives/45-Fixing-FreeNX-NoMachine-NX-keyboard-glitches-e.g.-ALTGr.html)

the server-Xserver has the same xmodmap as the client-Xserver.
