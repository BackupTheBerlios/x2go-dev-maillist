Dear all,

i've updated to MacOS X Lion and get the known Problem that "Rosetta" 
is needed.

I tried the Workaround

>
>Hello x2go mac os lion users, here is a workaround for using x2go 
> client on os x lion:
>
>1. Download and install OpenNX Client for Mac. 
> (http://sourceforge.net/projects/opennx/)
>2. Inside the opennx...dmg right click the OpenNX.pkg and click "show 
> package content"
>3. Doubleclick Contents/Archive.pax.gz
>4. Go inside Archive/Library/OpenNX
>5. Copy "bin" + "lib" + "share" to your clipboard (cmd-c)
>6. Go to your x2goclient.app
>7. Right click the x2goclient.app and click "show package content"
>8. Go to "Contents" folder
>9. Paste your clipboard inside this folder
>10. Copy the content inside the "bin" folder to the "exe" folder 
> (nxproxy will be overwritten)
>
>Now you can start x2goclient and connect to your server. If you get 
> this error: "Invalid MIT-MAGIC-COOKIE-1 key" click on X11, start a 
> terminal an type >"xhost +localhost".
>
>Regards
>
>Stephan Diehl
>Geschäftsführer
>
>Open Solutions GmbH & Co. KG
>Luisenweg 109
>20537 Hamburg
>
