One thing to note, though, when using --autostart=<app>:
<app> is the part of the file name before .desktop - NOT the display 
name as shown in the menu.
So, to autostart Icedove, assuming you have a file called 
icedove.desktop in /etc/x2go/applications, you'd call 
--autostart="icedove", even though it may be displayed as "Icedove" or 
"Mail Client" or some other name depending on your language setting.
