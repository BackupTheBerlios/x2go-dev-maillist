Hi!

Launchpad isn't Ubuntu specific.

You could enable the bug tracker feature for the x2go project which
would put all x2go bugs at:
https://launchpad.net/x2go/+bugs

And once x2go enters Ubuntu, the Ubuntu-specific bug reports will be at:
https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/x2go/+bugs

This allows easy linking of bugs between the upstream project
(https://launchpad.net/x2go) and the distributions packaging it (Ubuntu
or others) as Launchpad lets you easily link a bug to upstream bug
trackers.
