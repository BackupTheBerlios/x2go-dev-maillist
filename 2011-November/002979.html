Media playing is always discovering the bottlenecks of terminal server  
solutions. Actually, X2Go is not designed to fulfill the need of  
playing Youtube videos.

In professional setups I provided thin clients with a local browser  
and flash and use those local browser for meeting such use cases.

And: with proper hardware and network toplogy, videos do play within  
X2Go sessions, but only for a few users at once (1-2-3-4). So maybe  
there is really something wrong with your setup. To get help with  
that, please

   (1) subscribe to x2go-user mailing list and ask there (this is the
       developers' list and the list is not intended to discuss such issues)
   (2) send proper information on your X2Go setup
