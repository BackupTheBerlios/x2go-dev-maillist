As you can read in the (small)documentionfile of x2go.xsession 
(/usr/share/doc/x2goserver-xsession/doc/README),  the x-setup must be 
adapted. X2go.Xsession doesn't run "out of the box". First you should 
know how lxde (login- and displaymanager) and X11/Xsession working 
together. Then you will be able to configure x2go.xsession.

I have tested x2go.xsession with lubuntu 11.10 . It's working fine with 
every xsession (Lubuntu, Openbox, ...)

Sorry, but in this case,  it's no x2go problem.
