Hello all,

As you know, X2Go client on windows have problems with some antivirus
programs. I have invested some time in investigation of this problem. It
seems like QTcpSocket have problems if antivirus software running on the
machine. The closed sockets are not accessible directly after closing,
but only after finishing of owner process. I have replaced QTcpSocket
with WinSocks and it seems to work now. I have successfully tested it
with Avast. Please test it with your antivirus software and give your
feedback.

You can download a new version of client here:
http://code.x2go.org/releases/binary-win32/x2goclient/x2goclient-3.99.0.2-setup.exe
