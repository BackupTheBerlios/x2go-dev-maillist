> Any unobvious steps to repeat this?

No, just try crashing the client in the ways that have been described 
here. To sum them up:
- stopping/rebooting the server
- interrupting the network connection:
   - "soft" via ifconfig ifacename down/up on the server
   - "soft" via ifconfig ifacename down/up on the client
   - "hard" by pulling the cable
- activating the client-side screensaver and waiting a few minutes

Please cc: 100 at bugs.x2go.org when reporting your results so they get 
attached to the bug report.
