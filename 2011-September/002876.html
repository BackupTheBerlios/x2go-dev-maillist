Hi,

sounds reasonable to me. A few things that came to mind when reading the 
page (it it not a request to fix the - just a few thoughts):
* what happens to stdout and stderr? What is propagated to the 
client/logfile/</dev/null>?
* Why always return 0? At least for pre-* it might be nice if something 
can fail.
* What do you mean with "fail during execution"? Maybe something like 
"Return codes are ignored and failures are therefore not propagated" is 
easier to understand.
* Most other systems have two-digit ordering
* I'd make digit ordering the rule, not a suggestion
* What about the environment? What information do I get 
(client-string/version/username/remote ip)?

Cheers
Morty
