Package: x2goclient
Version: 4.0.1.2

On Windows and Linux, the X2Go Client tray icon behaves correctly:
left click: un-minimize/un-hide
right click: show the context menu

However, on the Mac X2Go Client, clicking on the menu bar icon (i.e,
the "tray icon") un-minimizes and then shows the context menu. In
other words, it does both actions. It does so whether you left-click
or right-click.

Observed on Mac OS X 10.9.1 Mavericks.
Observed with both a a mid-2011 macbook air's trackpad (where 2
fingers emulates a right click) and with a 2 button mouse.
