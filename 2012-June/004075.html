Oh, I have the very similar problem now: when i choose in settings to
show lxpanel at the bottom, i don't see it (it seems to me that it's
drawn beyond the visible screen area), although the process is alive
and working (Alt+F2 works, for example). If i choose to show it at the
top of the desktop, it draws ok.

resolution instead of fullscreen: x2goclient session window is
declared as resizeable and when you resize it, lxpanel resizes,
headers of maximized apps are resized too but the contents of their
windows are not drawn beyond the initial area.

I don't remember but it seems that everything is OK when you choose
fullscreen mode.
Also, i remember that original NXClient session window is NOT
resizeable when you select fixed resolution for session.
