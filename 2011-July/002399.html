Hi all,

Dick Kniep from the Lindix company in the Netherlands has sponsored  
the backporting of X2go server-side packages to Debian lenny.

As many of you still use lenny on the servers, I persuaded him to make  
this a contribution to the community.

That is: you can use this APT line to install recent X2go packages on  
Debian lenny:

deb http://packages.x2go.org/debian lenny main

NOTE: we will not backport the client-side packages. This is only for  
the X2go servers. Unfortunately there will neither be a backport of  
x2gokde3bindings, which has not been moved into X2go Git.

Greets,
