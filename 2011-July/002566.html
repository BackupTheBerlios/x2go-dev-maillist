I'm one of the users that have reported problems with x2goclient
(newer than 3.01-13) on Win 7 x64 and it turned out that the problem
was caused by Symantec Endpoint Protection (SEP). When I uninstalled
SEP, x2goclient started working. Seems like antivirus (and perhaps
firewall) software is the first thing to uninstall (not just disable,
really uninstall) when troubleshooting x2goclient on Windows.
