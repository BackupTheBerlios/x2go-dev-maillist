Package: x2gobroker
Version: git/master

After moving x2gobroker-authservice logging away from x2gobroker-daemon
logging, authservice now wants it's own logger config, by default called
/etc/x2go/broker/x2gobroker-authservice-logger.conf, but this file is not
installed by the deb-package and does not exist anywhere in git. This
hinders authservice from starting.
