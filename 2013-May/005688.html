Package: x2gobroker
Version: 0.0.2.2 (git)
Severity: wishlist

While trying to debug the problems I had, leading to submitting bug #216,
was quite painful. x2gobroker-agent dies silently if it can't run
x2gobroker-agent.pl and nothing giving even a hint of a problem gets logged
by x2gobroker to broker.log (on the broker, not the terminal server). It
would be very helpful if nothing was ever allowed to die silently, but
instead logged error messages.
I guess this goes for pretty much any part of x2go, but this bug is about
the particular case with x2gobroker-agent.
