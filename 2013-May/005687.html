Package: x2gobroker
Version: 0.0.2.2 (git)
Severity: critical

When I build debs (debuild -uc -us) for x2gobroker from git, after the
recent security fix to x2gobroker-agent, the resulting x2gobroker-agent
tries to run /usr/local/lib/x2go/x2gobroker-agent.pl instead of
/usr/lib/x2go/x2gobroker-agent.pl. This means that autologin with
x2gobroker does not work.
