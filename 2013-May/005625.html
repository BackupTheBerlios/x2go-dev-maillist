Hello,

I also get vcxsrv crashes with Chrome, to the extent that I consider Chrome unusable under x2go. In my case it's a read fault in the fbBlt function accessing freed memory. I filed a bug against vcxsrv (http://sourceforge.net/p/vcxsrv/bugs/11/ on vcxsrv's bug tracker, but I haven't heard back from the developer.

It's an interesting data point that vcxsrv doesn't crash in unaccelerated sessions. It sounds like nxproxy is feeding something unexpected to the x-server.
